Six Seasons in Bangladesh

Summer
Summer or summertime is the hottest and brightest of the four temperate seasons, occurring after spring and before autumn
Rainy
The rainy season is the time of year when a region experiences its most significant rainfall
Autumn
Autumn is the temperate season between summer and winter, characterized by cooler temperatures, shorter days, and colorful falling leaves
Late Autumn
Late autumn is the period of the season that comes at the end of autumn, typically in November and early December in many Northern Hemisphere locations.
Winter
Winter is one of the four seasons in the Northern Hemisphere, where it takes place from the beginning of December to the beginning of March.
Spring
"Spring" can refer to the season between winter and summer, the Spring Framework for software development, a source of water, or an elastic mechanical device.
